Producing Quasi-Definitive Data
===============================

.. include:: ../../appendices/appendices.rst


The production of quasi-definitive data is similar to the
process for definitive data, the difference being that
quasi-definitive data are submitted within 3 months of
acquisition. Provided quasi-definitive data are produced using
the best available baselines such that data meet the
specification given in |app_imag_term|, any method can be
used. Observatories typically adopt the same process to produce
quasi-definitive data as they do for definitive data as this
simplifies the production of definitive data at the end of
the year.
